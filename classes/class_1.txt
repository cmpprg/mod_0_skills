Class: Menu

Attributes:
color_scheme (string)
vegan_items (boolean)
recipes (array)

Methods:
read_menu (iterate through recipes and puts them to terminal)
change_color_scheme (change color_scheme attribute)
meat_day(toggle vegan_items attribute to false)
add_item(append item to recipes and add 1 to number_of_items)
